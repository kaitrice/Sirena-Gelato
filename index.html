Our Story

After decades of working in the hospitality industry, we sought out a new skill; the art of crafting gelato.
Taught by third generation Venetian dessert specialist, our family has been serving our signature gelato since opening in 2005 at our original home in the Fairhaven Historic District of Bellingham, WA. We strive too curate the highest quality gelato we can and make it right in the heart of Bellingham, WA. After perfecting our craftsmanship over the years, we opened our second storefront in Downtown Kirkland in 2011.
