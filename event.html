Tips

On your scheduled pick up day, come to your closest Sirena Gelato location and bring a cooler. Dry ice is very helpful during long transportation and outdoor events (grocery stores for approximately $1.00/lb) If your event has a freezer, no dry ice is needed.
Coolers are available for purchase at Sirena Gelato locations

Charities

We love filtering our dollars directly into our community by partnering with local non-profits, schools, charities, and educational groups.

So many wonderful groups in our community have recieved our donations. We give out gift certificates and coupons often; contact us with details of your request today!

To help raise additional money . . .

Simply pick a day (Monday, Tuesday, or Wednesday) for your event at one of our Sirena Gelato locations. Inform everyone in your organization, friends/family, social media about your fundraiser. If you would like to bring decorations for your fundraiser up to a day beforehand so that regular customers can see and some support!

Within a week of your event, you will receive a check for 15% of the total gelato sales during the event!

All we need is your 501(c)(3) non profit number to get started!
